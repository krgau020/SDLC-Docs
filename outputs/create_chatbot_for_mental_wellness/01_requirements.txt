## **Original Client Requirements**

## **Refined Functional Requirements**

**1. Overall Goals & Objectives:**

*   **FR1.1:** The chatbot shall provide accessible and supportive mental wellness assistance to users 24/7.
*   **FR1.2:** The chatbot shall improve user’s self-awareness and coping skills related to mental wellbeing.
*   **FR1.3:** The chatbot shall offer immediate support and resources during mental health crises.
*   **FR1.4:** The chatbot shall maintain user privacy and data security adhering to relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA - depending on target audience and location).

**2. User Interaction & Functionality:**

*   **FR2.1:** The chatbot shall initiate a conversation with a welcoming and non-judgmental greeting.
*   **FR2.2:** The chatbot shall support natural language understanding (NLU) to interpret user input, including variations in phrasing and sentiment.
*   **FR2.3:** The chatbot shall provide responses in a conversational and empathetic tone, avoiding overly clinical language.
*   **FR2.4:** The chatbot shall offer a range of pre-defined responses addressing common mental wellness topics, including:
    *   **FR2.4.1:** Stress management techniques (e.g., deep breathing, mindfulness exercises).
    *   **FR2.4.2:** Anxiety reduction strategies.
    *   **FR2.4.3:** Mood tracking and journaling prompts.
    *   **FR2.4.4:** Information about common mental health conditions (e.g., depression, anxiety disorders). *Note: This information should be presented as educational and not as a diagnosis.*
*   **FR2.5:** The chatbot shall allow users to express their feelings and emotions freely without fear of judgment.
*   **FR2.6:** The chatbot shall provide personalized recommendations based on user input and mood tracking data (if integrated).
*   **FR2.7:** The chatbot shall support multi-turn conversations, maintaining context and remembering previous interactions.

**3. Crisis Detection & Support:**

*   **FR3.1:** The chatbot shall utilize sentiment analysis to detect indicators of a mental health crisis (e.g., suicidal ideation, self-harm).
*   **FR3.2:** Upon detecting a potential crisis, the chatbot shall immediately offer support resources, including:
    *   **FR3.2.1:** Contact information for crisis hotlines (e.g., Suicide Prevention Lifeline, Crisis Text Line).
    *   **FR3.2.2:** Links to mental health organizations and websites.
    *   **FR3.2.3:**  Option to connect with a human support professional (if available and integrated).
*   **FR3.3:** The chatbot shall log all crisis detection events and user interactions for auditing and improvement purposes (while maintaining user privacy).

**4. Mood Tracking Integration (Optional):**

*   **FR4.1:** The chatbot shall integrate with a basic mood tracking system (user-provided or built-in).
*   **FR4.2:** The chatbot shall allow users to log their mood on a daily basis.
*   **FR4.3:** The chatbot shall analyze mood data to identify trends and patterns.
*   **FR4.4:** The chatbot shall provide personalized insights and recommendations based on mood data.

**5. Platform & Security:**

*   **FR5.1:** The chatbot shall be accessible via a web interface and a mobile application (iOS and Android).
*   **FR5.2:** All user data and conversations shall be encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols (e.g., TLS/SSL).
*   **FR5.3:** The chatbot shall comply with relevant data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
*   **FR5.4:** The chatbot shall implement robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access and data breaches.

**6. Analytics & Reporting:**

*   **FR6.1:** The chatbot shall track user engagement metrics, including:
    *   **FR6.1.1:** Number of active users.
    *   **FR6.1.2:** Conversation duration.
    *   **FR6.1.3:** Frequency of use.
    *   **FR6.1.4:**  Most frequently asked questions.
*   **FR6.2:** The chatbot shall generate reports on user engagement and identify areas for improvement.
*   **FR6.3:**  Data collected for analytics shall be anonymized to protect user privacy.

**7. Error Handling & Fallback:**

*   **FR7.1:** The chatbot shall gracefully handle unexpected user input or errors.
*   **FR7.2:** The chatbot shall provide helpful error messages to the user.
*   **FR7.3:**  If the chatbot is unable to understand a user’s request, it shall offer alternative options or direct the user to a human support professional (if available).
